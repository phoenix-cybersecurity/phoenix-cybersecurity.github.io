Florida Poly Cybersecurity Club

What We Do

Learning

We host weekly meetings where we cover a diverse range of Computing and Security topics. We have presentations led by Students, Professors, and other guest speakers.

Labs

Whenever we're able to, we give attendees the oppurtunity to get hands on experience on whatever we're covering: virtual networks to hack, locks to pick, ciphers to crack, and much more...

Programming

We host weekly programming meetings for those hungry to improve their skills, learn optimization strategies, language tricks, and collaborate with others on interesting projects...

CTF

Our club has a Capture the Flag team, where we compete against other teams to solve challenges in Cryptography, Reverse Engineering, Software Exploitation, Forensics, and many more...
